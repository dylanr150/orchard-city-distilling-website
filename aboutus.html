The People Behind the Pour

Meet the Owner

Orchard City Distilling is a fully licensed commercial distillery operating out of West Kelowna in the beautiful Okanagan Valley. Purchased by a group of friends in 2015, Orchard City has grown to have over 50 products registered with the BCLDB.

Our decision to purchase bulk alcohol rather than distill on site has given us the flexibility to take on any project. It also leaves us more time to focus on helping our clients build unique and successful brands.

— Kristian Soransen, Owner
