How We Work

The Process

Orchard City is set up to help our clients take care of the entire registration process to get your product licensed within BC and available to order province wide. Whether it’s a spirit in a bottle or refreshment beverage in a can, Orchard City has the knowledge and experience to help you navigate through this complicated process.

We are here to assist in research and development of your beverage, designing and developing your brand, and most importantly getting on shelves!

Orchard City Distilling maintains a Commercial Distillery license as well as an Excise Spirits and Warehousing license allowing us to purchase and store bulk alcohol. No project is too big or small. We currently work closely with industry leading large scale co-packers to make sure we can handle any size project.

Orchard City’s goal is to develop long lasting and prosperous relationships with our clients. Communication is key when trying to develop and launch a brand and Orchard City prides itself in that very thing. Always available, and quick to respond.
